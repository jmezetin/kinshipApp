Kinship Friends and Family

SDA Kinship’s family community is diverse and includes experienced and supportive family members: parents, pastors, church administrators, and other professionals, all having a common bond—an LQBTIQ+ family member. While walking, or having walked this path before, members of SDA Kinship’s family community have used their experience and knowledge to develop strong, safe paths of communication between themselves and their LGBTIQ+ family members, even when disagreements linger.

About Us

John & Carolyn have been involved with the SDA Marriage Encounter program since April 1977. They have supported over 250 weekend workshop including presenting and leading in over 130 workshops. They have interfaced and worked with over 4,000 couples. Those workshops focused on helping couples learn to share and communicate facts, feelings and emotions. We have seen thousands and thousands of couples improve their understandings of each other and grow stronger and more loving relationships.

John and Carolyn Wilt have served on the Kinship Board since 2015.
